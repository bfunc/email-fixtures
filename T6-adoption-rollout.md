# T6 — Adoption tracking and rollout

## Why

Adoption is gradual and team-driven, so progress must be visible and the
finish line concrete.

## What

- A visible counter of remaining legacy usages (per area where possible),
  based on the T1 grandfather list.
- Announce the paved road to the teams once the cookbook and first examples
  are merged; optional walkthrough session.
- Explicitly: mass migration and legacy deletion are out of this epic. A
  future decommission epic starts when the counter reaches zero.

## Done when

The counter is visible, teams are informed, and the exit criterion for the
future decommission epic is written down.
