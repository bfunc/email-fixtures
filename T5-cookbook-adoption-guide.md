# T5 — Cookbook and adoption guide

## Why

The migration is many similar small changes done by different people over
time. Ready-made recipes make each one a short copy/adapt job and keep the
results uniform.

## What

Authored by Pavel:

- A before/after recipe for every pattern in T4, each linked to its merged
  reference example.
- A short "migrating your area" guide: definition of done, testing
  checklist, who to ask.

## Done when

The cookbook is published in the repo docs and every recipe points to a
merged example.
